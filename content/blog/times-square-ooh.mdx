## Why Times Square Is the World's OOH Capital

Times Square attracts over **330,000 daily visitors** and is viewed by an estimated **1.5 million digital impressions per day**. Its iconic LED billboards have become synonymous with global brand prestige, making it the most sought-after outdoor advertising location on the planet.

## Types of Times Square Billboards

### 1. Spectacular LED Displays

- **Size**: Up to 25,000 sq ft (e.g., the Nasdaq Tower)
- **Format**: Full-motion video, 3D anamorphic content
- **Best for**: Global brand launches, entertainment premieres
- **Cost**: $50,000 – $4,000,000/month depending on size and position

### 2. Digital Bulletin Boards

- **Size**: 14' × 48' standard digital format
- **Format**: Static or 8-second rotation
- **Best for**: Regional campaigns, product awareness
- **Cost**: $10,000 – $50,000/month

### 3. Wrapped Building Facades

- **Size**: Custom, often covering entire building sides
- **Format**: Vinyl wrap or mesh banner
- **Best for**: Fashion, luxury, movie releases
- **Cost**: $100,000 – $500,000 for a 4-week run

## Campaign Strategy Tips

1. **Duration matters** — A 2-week minimum is recommended for brand recall. Shorter "blitz" campaigns (1–3 days) work for event-driven buzz.
2. **Content for scale** — Design for distance: large type, high contrast, minimal copy. Viewers see your ad from 200+ feet away.
3. **Social amplification** — 70% of Times Square campaigns are shared on social media. Include hashtags or AR triggers to maximize organic reach.
